The Abyss

The Abyss has never been so alive as it is now. The abyss is a dark place located in a gloomy environment in Greater Buenos Aires. Embark on a bloody journey through the sinister open world of the abyss THE PATH INTO THE ABYSS takes full advantage of the potential of Unreal Engine, delivering cutting-edge graphics and unparalleled immersion into the game world. Its advanced artificial intelligence system forces the survivor to plan ahead when approaching enemies. The world simulation system creates a comprehensive environment in which the player's actions alter the abyss.Monster and humans won't be the only dangers you'll face. You will witness the challenges that survivors experienced while consuming scarce supplies, such as food, drink, medication or alcohol.
